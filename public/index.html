What is KAEMS?

Karnataka Arya Ediga Mahila Sangha (KAEMS) is an NGO that aims for the upliftment and empowerment of families as a system with special focus on women and children.

The Sangha was registered in 1988 with the noble cause of service to work towards achievement of goals for the economically backward women in society.

With the above goal in focus, the Sangha has been carrying out certain activities for the target population since its inception.

What do we do?

Welfare Activities

Sangha had been instrumental in donating Sewing machines to the Disabled and women from extreme lower economic background. Free tailoring classes have been conducted for them. Programmes have been conducted to encourage self-employment among women and self-employment camps have been conducted. The Sangha has also donated chilli Powder and masala Powder Machines among them. The Sangha has distributed clothes and books to the economically disadvantaged, mainly as part of relief operations in disaster such as earthquakes, Tsunami and floods. Public talks on domestic Violence against women, Nano (Nutritional supplement) on stress management, home gardening LIC etc have been conducted by the Sangha for the target population. A matrimonial cell has been established so as to help the families in finding appropriate alliance and family court to settle domestic and matrimonial disputes. The Sangha has also donated money for mass marriages among the economically disadvantaged people.

Socio Economic Programmes

Skill development programmes are conducted regularly. Vocational training is provided in various field.

Consumer Awareness

Consumer awareness Programmes are conducted for consumer rights/ laws and protection.

Counseling

We maintain a counseling cell to help solve marital, family or personal disputes. Such disputes are common among low-income families and have a considerable impact on their livelihoods. Solving these issues and keeping them at bay ensures a happy environment which is the key to personal development.

Empowering women in rural areas

Despite efforts from the government rural areas in India remain largely untouched by societal developments and need help accelerating the proccess. Our programmes in these areas ensure the overall social development of women.

Health Sector

Sangha has conducted medical camps for the target population, mainly Eye Camps and on Mental Health of Women and Children. Every year, the Sangha donates money to NIMHANS for so as to ensure optimum patient care services through poor patients funds, which distributed free medicines for patients. Donation towards aids and appliances such as Wheel chair is also done. Every year, the sangha give donation to Cancer patients and cardiac patients from lower economic status. The members visit Old age Homes regularly and conduct recreational activities as well as distribute prizes and fruits. Every year on auspicious days such as Rajyotsava and Children’s day, the Sangha conduct sports competitions and cultural programme for children and elderly ladies and felicitate the winners.

Old Age Pension

To help women who are senior citizens, or women who are poor and deserted by the family, we started the old age pension scheme. We identify their needs and extend financial aid every month which would help them take care of their daily, basic needs from food to shelter and their health.

Scholarship

Education is absolutely necessary for women empowerment. Realizing this, at the start of our Sangha way back in 1988, we decided that awarding of scholarship to deserving girl students would be our prime project. The fact that this project has grown immensely over the years, is a matter of pride to our Sangha. In the last two and a half decades, the scholarship from our Sangha has helped about five hundred girls in their education.

Every year we grant scholarship to meritorious girls studying for a diploma, degree, post graduation, engineering, and medicine. Students from rural areas get more priority. We receive application from all over the state. Keeping economically backward areas in mind, we disperse the scholarship taking all the districts into consideration.

This project has so far been funded by the committee members and invitee members and a few donors. It is our dream to make every girl in the community educated. We also see a change in the young girls. There is an increased enthusiasm among them to go for higher studies. They score very high marks that it becomes difficult to select the deserving students. In such a scenario, we wish to reach out to more students for which we need more funds. We are making an impassioned appeal to large-hearted donors to make our dream a reality.

Cultural Events and Competitions

We periodically conduct cultural activates to recognize and encourage the hidden talents among our women and also to inculatate healthy competitive spirit.

Felicitations

Members of KAEMS are honoured with various felicitations and awards to recognize their achievements and contributions to the society. This helps upkeep morale among other members and creates a healthy, competitive environment that generates much needed motivation within the community.

Matrimonial Cell

Due to popular demand from many parents who find it difficult to find an appropriate match for their children in the community, we came up with the idea of a matrimonial cell which has been running successfully for two decades. We hope that a large number of people from our community will use this facility. We guarantee easy profile registration, 100% privacy, and manual profile validation and screening.

Events

General Meetings

We conduct regular meetings to asses the impact of our reach and efforts and create a roadmap to our future activities.

We maintain an in-house journal called "BIMBA" devoted to articles related to women's issues and reports of the local branch's activities.

Picnics

We believe various functions and get togethers promote a sense of camaraderie among our members and is a strong adhesive that binds us together.

Sports Meets

Sports meets are held once a year to bring out the sportsmanship of our members and their families.

Commitee Member Meetings

We hold regular commitee meetings to ensure the year-round smooth functioning of KAEMS.

Hostels

KAEMS started their hostel for girl students in the year 1991 in a rented house in the Bengaluru and Shivamogga. Later in the year 2007 We established a hostel at Sheshderipuram and is running successfully catering to financially backward girls in a safe and secure environment. Thousands on such girls from various parts of Karnataka have been benefited and are now well placed individuals in various government and private and MNC organization.

We have been then funded by social welfare dept. for construction. We have also spent large amount in maintaining the building. We started with 25 girls and at present 55 girls are availing this facility. Students from various parts of Karnataka perusing various educational courses have benefited from our hostel. We feel proud that more than 1000 students have availed our hostel facility since its inception. Smooth functioning of the hostel was possible due to whole hearted contribution from our Committee Members, donors & philanthropic from our community and society in large.
